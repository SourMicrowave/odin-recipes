Oven Cooked Chicken

Oven fried cripsy chicken recipe made with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Combine the flour with salt and pepper to taste. Put bread crumbs in a seperate bowl.
  3. Beat the eggs in another bowl.
  4. Coat the chicken pieces in the flour, then the egg, then the bread crumbs.
  5. Pour the oil into a large baking dish. Add the breaded chicken thighs to the baking dish and sprinkle with paprika.
  6. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes. Then turn the pieces over and bake for another 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve. YOU WILL ENJOY.
