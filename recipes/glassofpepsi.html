A Glass of Pepsi

A glass of pepsi, nothing else.

Ingredients

  • A glass
  • Some pepsi
  • Optional: Ice

Steps

  1. Put the glass on any flat surface
  2. Open your pepsi source (can, bottle, tap??)
  3. Pour the pepsi into the glass until it is almost full
  4. Optional: Add your ice to the glass
  5. DRINK IT.
